Gratin de pâtes jambon-fromage à l'italienne

Discover this ham and cheese pasta gratin recipe! Ultra-delicious and comforting, this budget-friendly recipe is simple and quick to make, much to the delight of children.

Ingredients

  • Pasta 250g
  • Milk 25cl
  • Grated Gruyere cheese
  • Flour 1tbsp
  • Pepper
  • Thick crème fraîche 20cl
  • White ham 3 slices
  • Onion 1
  • Nutmeg
  • Garlic 1 clove

Preparation

Cut the ham into small squares and finely chop the onion. Sweat the onion in a knob of butter (incorporating a little oil to avoid burning the butter). Meanwhile, boil the water for the pasta.

Then sprinkle the onions with a little flour (usually about a tablespoon), to absorb all the fat. Cook until a biscuit smell is released (make a blond roux). Lower the heat or the temperature of the plate then add the milk, mix well and add the crème fraîche. Mix again, grate a little nutmeg on top, pepper. At the same time, when the pasta water boils, immerse the spaghetti for 6 minutes only.

Add the ham to the mixture, mix, then add the Gruyère: the quantity is enough to make the mixture a little sticky.

To finish

Once the sauce and pasta are ready, rub a clove of garlic on a gratin dish and pour the pasta in, then cover everything with sauce. Sprinkle with grated Gruyère cheese, and brown everything under the grill (be careful, watch carefully).

C'est terminé
