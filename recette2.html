Shepherd's pie: the best recipe

      Shepherd’s Pie is a comforting dish made with minced meat, often lamb, topped with mashed potatoes.
Easy and flavorful, this classic combines seasoned
meat and vegetables, all cooked under a golden crust.
A must-have for family meals!

Ingredients

  • Pasta 250g
  • Milk 25cl
  • Grated Gruyere cheese
  • Flour 1tbsp
  • Pepper
  • Thick crème fraîche 20cl
  • White ham 3 slices
  • Onion 1
  • Nutmeg
  • Garlic 1 clove
